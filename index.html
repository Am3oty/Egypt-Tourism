Alexandria is an ancient Egyptian city on the Mediterranean, rich in history and culture.

Aswan is a serene Egyptian city on the Nile, known for its beauty and ancient monuments.

Asyut is a historic city in central Egypt, known for its ancient sites and cultural heritage.

Beheira is an Egyptian governorate in the Nile Delta, known for agriculture and historic sites.

Beni Suef is an Egyptian city on the Nile, known for agriculture and archaeological sites.

Cairo, Egypt's capital, is a bustling city known for its history, culture, and landmarks like the Pyramids and the Nile.

Dakahlia is an Egyptian governorate in the Nile Delta, known for agriculture and vibrant cities like Mansoura.

Damietta is a coastal Egyptian city, known for its port, furniture industry, and delicious seafood.

Faiyum is an Egyptian governorate southwest of Cairo, known for its ancient history, lakes, and agricultural richness.

Gharbia is an Egyptian governorate in the Nile Delta, known for its agricultural production and historical landmarks.

Giza is an Egyptian city famous for the Great Pyramids, the Sphinx, and its rich ancient history.

Ismailia is a city in Egypt, located along the Suez Canal, known for its scenic gardens, historical significance, and strategic location.
